ABOUT US:-

The North-Eastern Himalayan regions of India, Nepal & Bhutan have been our primary focus to form Offbeat Tours 365. Offbeat Tours 365 is Specialist based in Darjeeling District in West Bengal with its branches at Gangtok (Sikkim). Its operational area stretches across the Trans Himalayan region with a strong focus to the less visited East Indian States like Sikkim, West Bengal and it also covers the neighboring countries like Nepal & Bhutan. North East India is a promising Travel destination with its unique landscape, beauty, culture & diversity. The scope of experience and activity travel is immense in this part of the world when the tourists are looking for new ideas beyond simple visits to popular tourist circuits. Here at Offbeat Tours 365 Custom-made arrangements can be made for Independent Travelers and Special Interest Groups. If you do not find your travel ideal among the per-scheduled itineraries, Offbeat Tours 365 can quickly and easily develop a tour which meets your requirements – in destination, duration, cost and other activities. For further information, please feel free to contact us, we would love to cater your needs…
